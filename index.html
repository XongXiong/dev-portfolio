About Me

Hello! My name is Xong Xiong and I am a full stack software developer. I am originally from Oklahoma and have moved to Minnesota in June of 2017. It was at this point in my life where I felt the need to better myself in order to have a sense of self-fulfillment.

In the past, I worked in the food and retail industries. I've always had an interest in the tech industry as I had wanted to do IT when I was younger. Realizing that the hardware side of computers wasn't right for me, I decided to go towards the software route. I found myself at Prime Digital Academy after moving to Minnesota where I learned the full stack of software development mainly focused on the MEAN stack.

Going to Prime changed my life forever and I am now an aspiring developer. My current goal is to find a company that will challenge me to apply my skills in hopes of gaining even more knowledge about the world of software developing.
